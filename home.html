Sitting disease! How can sitting be harmful? Well, inactivity can increase your chances for heart disease, obesity among other ailments. On average an American worker spends over 55 hours a week sitting. So much inactivity can also disturb your sleep cycle, damage memory and shorten your life!! So, we gotta GETUP&MOVE.
As post-bacc students, with full-time jobs, most of us have very little time and we spend a lot of it on our duffs. I wondered, surely, I could find some time to work out? Turns out, I did find, maybe 10 minutes here, 20 minutes there. BUT, not enough time to drive to the gym.
So, I decided to collect information over the break and here it is!!
Listed on this website are resources that I have found through online searches, talking with friends who are avid gym clients, my old gym class teacher, and several yoga & fitness instructors.
