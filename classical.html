Stay Flexible

Reminder to GET UP, STAND UP in minutes

Ballet dancers are strong and flexible. The strength and flexibility comes from working on individual muscle groups and the repetitive motions will have an impact on increasing flexibility. I have grouped the resources so that depending on the time available you can choose to do any segment. Remember to exercise caution if you are ill or cold.
